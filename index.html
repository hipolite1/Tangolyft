Designed for liveable cities.

TangoLyft helps reduce congestion and parking pressure by making it easier to share rides instead of owning more cars.

  • Smart routing that helps avoid bottlenecks and busy streets.
  • Options for off-peak discounts to spread demand across the day.
  • Data insights that can help partners understand urban mobility trends.
